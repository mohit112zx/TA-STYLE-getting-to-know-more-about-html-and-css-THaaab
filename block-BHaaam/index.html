About Dogs

The dog is a pet animal. A dog has sharp teeth so that it can eat flesh very easily,
it has four legs, two ears, two eyes, a tail, a mouth, and a nose. It is a very clever animal
and is very useful in catching thieves. It runs very fast, barks loudly and attacks the strangers.
A dog saves the life of the master from danger. One can find dogs everywhere in the world. Dogs are
a very faithful animal. It has a sharp mind and a strong sense of hearing smelling the things.
It also has many qualities like swimming in the water, jumping from anywhere, good smelling sense.
